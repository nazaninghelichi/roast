# Business Idea Evaluation Rubric

## 1. Problem Clarity (0-10)
- 10: Urgent, specific problem affecting many people
- 7-9: Clear problem, could be more precisely defined
- 4-6: Problem exists but vague or niche
- 1-3: Poorly defined, not a real pain point
- 0: No clear problem being solved

## 2. Market Size (0-10)
- 10: Massive market (>$1B TAM), growing fast
- 7-9: Large market (>$100M TAM), stable or growing
- 4-6: Medium market ($10M-$100M TAM)
- 1-3: Small or unclear market
- 0: No identifiable market

## 3. Feasibility (0-10)
- 10: Buildable with existing technology, low barriers
- 7-9: Technically feasible, minor challenges
- 4-6: Significant technical or regulatory hurdles
- 1-3: Major feasibility concerns
- 0: Not feasible with current technology

## 4. Differentiation (0-10)
- 10: Unique, no direct competitors, defensible moat
- 7-9: Differentiated with competitive advantages
- 4-6: Competitive market, some differentiation
- 1-3: Crowded market, unclear differentiation
- 0: Identical to existing solutions

## 5. Revenue Potential (0-10)
- 10: Clear monetization, strong unit economics, scalable
- 7-9: Good monetization path, reasonable margins
- 4-6: Monetization possible but unclear
- 1-3: Difficult to monetize
- 0: No viable revenue model
